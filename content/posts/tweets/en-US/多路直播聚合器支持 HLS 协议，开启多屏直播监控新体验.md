- **3×3 Grid**: 9 screens intensive monitoring, information maximization
- **Horizontal Split**: Horizontal comparison, suitable for widescreen monitors

- **Vertical Stacking**: 1×4 vertical layout, flexible and changeable

- 🎬 **Real-time Switching**: Play/Pause/Refresh, one-click control
- 🔊 **Independent Volume**: Each live stream independently adjustable, supports vertical volume bars

- 📺 **Full-screen Support**: Single stream full-screen immersive viewing
- 📊 **Status Monitoring**: Connection/Playing/Error status displayed in real-time
- 🔄 **Automatic Reconnection**: Automatically attempts to reconnect when live stream is interrupted (maximum 3 times)
